Art Deco

Distinguished by its geometric shapes, bold colors, and luxurious materials, Art Deco emerged in the 1920s and 1930s. As a style of interior design, Art Deco is known for its emphasis on opulence and glamour, incorporating resources like chrome, glass, and exotic woods, as well as bold geometric patterns. This style is known for its emphasis on luxury, glamour, and sophistication, and is a popular choice for those seeking to add a touch of opulence to their homes.
